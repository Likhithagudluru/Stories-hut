Alert

Account security and avoiding scams

Avoid Publisher Scams. Stories Hut will never ask you to share sensitive information such as your Stories Hut password or full banking details outside your Stories Hut account. Stories Hut is a free service and not affiliated with third-party companies. Learn more about account security and avoiding scams.

New

Identity verification is rolling out to authors

We're committed to protecting those who publish with us and those who buy books on Stories Hut. As part of this effort, we are beginning to ask authors and publishers to verify their identity on Stories Hut. For details, visit our Help article.
